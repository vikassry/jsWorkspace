BATTLESHIP GAME
Help Section

It is a two player game and can be played on two different computers. Each player have five ships and two grids, first ocean grid where he can place his ships and second one is target grid where he can call-out a shoot to hit opponent's ship. Each ship has fixed size and to sunk that ship player has to attack on that ship as many time as size of that ship. Who will destroy all of his opponent ship will be the winner of the game. List of ship involved in this game.

Name Size
Battleship 4
Cruiser 3
Carrier 5
Destroyer 2
Submarine 3

Please go through the folowing instructioin if you are playing this game for first time.


  1. For placing a ship, you have to click on any box within the ocean-grid.

  2. The you can place the ships horizontally or vertically.

  3. The positions where ships are placed will be in green color.

  4. The you has to say ready as soon as deployed all the ships. This ready button will appear after deployment of all ship.

  5. After saying ready, you will get the target grid.

  6. you has to wait till the opponent you is ready.

  7. Turn will be displayed on the screen. According to that you can shoot on target grid.

  8. If you locates the opponent's ship correctly, then it will be a hit; otherwise it will be a miss. Hit and miss will be represented in red and green respectively.

  9. Each player will get alternative turns.

Victory

The player who successfully locates all the opponent's ships first by hitting each square they occupy, is the winner as all ships have been destroyed.
